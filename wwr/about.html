Exciting Expedition

Our company exist to ensure you and your family (or friends) gets the best experience in rafting. We also look out for your safety, making sure nobody gets hurt when you have fun rafting.

History

Exciting Expedition started in 1998 when my buddy Gyro and I came up with an idea to make rafting fun and safe for everyone. Now we serve hundreds of tourist looking for a fun and safe rafting with their friends and family.
